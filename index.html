In Japan, there is a dish traditionally
eaten on January 7th, at the beginning
of the year

It's called

Nanakusa-Gayu

or Seven-Herb Rice Porridge


Seven-Herb Porridge is made by chopping up herbs and boiling them together with rice until it becomes a thick porridge. Different regions may replace some of the herbs with more local ones or add meat to their porridges.
